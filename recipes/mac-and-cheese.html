Mac and Cheese

The Sher

Description

This is the recipe for making mac and cheese with ground beef. This recipe is from a friend The Sher.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of mac and cheese
  • Beef sausage

Sauce

  • Whipped cream
  • Sour cream
  • 2 choices of cheese (cheddar, swiss, mozzarella, montery jack, parmesean)

Seasoning

  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Basil (for yellow sauce)
  • Red pepper flakes (for yellow sauce)
  • Chipotle powder (for yellow sauce)

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni in rolling water for 10 minutes or until desired tenderness
  2. Brown the beef sausage in low heat to retain the fat until the sausage is cooked
  3. Pour in whipped cream, sour cream, and one handful of choice of cheese and let melt. The type and amount of cheese determines if you're making a white or yellow sauce. Add additional desired cheese and let melt. This is the base sauce.
  4. Add the seasonings for desired sauce and taste for desired sauce
  5. Mix macaroni and browned sausage with sauce
  6. Serve when hot
